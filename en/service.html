All-in-one service

Dressland customers only have to deal with the enjoyable aspects of ordering clothing or merchandising articles: choosing a design, specifying a product palette, judging prototypes and, obviously, wearing the outfits. Dressland handles all the rest: monitoring certified suppliers, producing flawless clothing, warehousing and on-time worldwide delivery.
Best of all, its processes are straightforward and efficient thanks to strategic partnerships with global logistics specialists and state-of-the-art apparel software and branding tools..

Ordering
made easy
ZugSeil's state-of-the-art web-based software is the brain behind the order process. This is a highly intuitive application that any of your employees can use without much training. It enables the simple fulfilment of your requirements and the efficient, lean and cost-effective administration of your purchasing and material management processes..

On the order platform, you can manage data such as employee clothing sizes, place individual or bulk orders and check your budget limits. Thanks to the MRP lists, you can minimize inventories to achieve significant cost savings.
Logistics
for any need
We let you decide how much logistics you need based on strategic partnerships with reputable logistics providers. After the articles are produced, they are invoiced EXW (ex-works) and can then be handled according to your instructions. We offer the following options:
  • Orders are broken down by office or country and then shipped to individual offices or national subsidiaries.
  • Articles are centrally stored in continental warehouses and shipped to national subsidiaries (or other organizational units that you define)
  • Clothing and merchandise inventories are managed in real time and orders are placed automatically (optional).
  • Items are centrally stored in continental warehouses and possibly regional distribution centres and are shipped and invoiced directly to individual employees or customers (residential addresses can also be used).
